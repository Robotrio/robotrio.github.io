About

In the year 2011, three robots get into more adventures than they bargained for. The three are Ace McNacho, Private Detective Bot; DANT-1C, a young nature expert; and Castilles, a bright-eyed (though not too bright) racer; and they soon find themselves among a series of increasingly strange events, the facts of which seem not to line up.

Robotrio is an illustrated audio drama, with episodes released both in audio-only podcast form and as a YouTube series. Season 1 airs on June 23, 2021, kicking off the series. The series Robotrio is in no way affiliated with the organ trio ROBOTRIO. Some content may be inappropriate for young audiences.

Meet the Crew

Full team

Robotrio is directed by Lei Valtapaz, with Kris Stasko, Kogan Productions, and WarriorStudios serving as head writers. Lei also serves as an artist, video/audio editor, and voice artist for Adelpha. Other artists include WarriorStudios, Amber, Carter H., and Rose the Bot.

Nicolas Hartman, Kris Stasko, and Joseph Esposito voice the three robots (Ace, Dant, and Castilles, respectively), while STARBREAKER voices the mysterious Deck. Krows Nest VA voices Ace's creator Thomas A. Edison IV, and Nicolas Hartman also voices Hector. Mxyl Valtapaz serves as both designer and developer for this site, and has done writups and technical work for promotional material.
